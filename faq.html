Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be:

  • In original, unused condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by the original receipt

Perishables, intimate apparel, and digital downloads are non-returnable. Sale items are final sale unless defective.

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days:

Credit/Debit Card5–10 business days
Mobile Money1–3 business days
PayPal3–5 business days
Store CreditInstant
  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"

  4. Print the prepaid return label (for eligible orders)
  5. Drop off at any partner courier location
