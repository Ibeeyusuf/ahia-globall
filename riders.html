What You Need to Join

Minimal requirements — we focus on dependability and attitude.

Personal Requirements

  • Age 18 years or older
  • Valid National ID or NIN (BVN acceptable)
  • Smartphone (Android 8.0+ or iOS 13+)
  • Bank account or registered mobile money wallet
  • Good knowledge of local roads and areas

Vehicle Requirements

  • Working bicycle, motorcycle or car/van
  • Valid vehicle registration documents
  • Valid motorcycle/driving licence (for motorized vehicles)
  • Third-party vehicle insurance
  • Delivery bag or box (we provide on request)

No vehicle? We have partnerships with motorcycle leasing companies. Ask about our Rider Starter Programme — ride with a leased bike and pay back from earnings with zero upfront cost.
