How to Take Your Measurements

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal and parallel to the floor.

Waist

Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, usually about 1 inch above your belly button.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, usually about 7–9 inches below your natural waist.

Inside Leg

Measure from the crotch seam down the inner leg to the ankle bone while standing straight.

Foot Length

Place your foot on paper, trace the outline, and measure from the heel to the longest toe.

Still Unsure?

Contact our support team with your measurements and we'll help you pick the right size.

Pro Tip — Between Sizes?

If you're between sizes, we recommend going up a size for a more comfortable fit, especially for outerwear and traditional styles. All AhiaGlobal products include the seller's specific sizing chart on the product page.
